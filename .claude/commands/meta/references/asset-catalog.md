## Composition Rules

1. **One mascot by default** — only use multiple if explicitly requested
2. **Always include a background** — gradients preferred over flat
3. **Pattern is optional** — adds texture but not always needed; skip for "clean" look
4. **Stamps are accent only** — small, decorative, never more than 1-2
5. **Text must be readable** — dark text on light backgrounds, white text on dark backgrounds
6. **Don't overcrowd** — text on the left, mascot on the right, leave breathing room
7. **Scale mascots proportionally** — use the native aspect ratio, target 350-500px bounding box
8. **Bottom-align mascots** — `y = 600 - scaled_height` keeps them grounded
