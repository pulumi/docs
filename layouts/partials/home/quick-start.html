Get Started in 5 Minutes

No complex setup. No credit card required. Just install the CLI and deploy your first stack.

1

Install Pulumi

$ curl -fsSL https://get.pulumi.com | sh

Or use brew, chocolatey, or download directly. Works on macOS, Linux, and Windows.

2

Create Your Project

$ pulumi new aws-typescript

Choose from templates for AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, and more. Use TypeScript, Python, Go, C#, or Java.

3

Deploy to the Cloud

$ pulumi up

Preview your changes, then deploy. Pulumi handles the rest. Your infrastructure is now code!
