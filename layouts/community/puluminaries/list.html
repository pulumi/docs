The Puluminaries program recognizes members of the Pulumi community who are experts and leaders in the field of programming and infrastructure, but also who give back to the community to make it grow! They help others get started, give feedback to make Pulumi’s roadmap even better, and educate people through content or public speaking. The program is open to all members of the community, and we welcome anyone to join the program.
